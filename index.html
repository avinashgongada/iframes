dosa resipe

Ingredients Dosa is made from a fermented batter of ground rice and lentils, such as urad dal or chana dal.

Preparation The batter is soaked overnight, then ground and fermented again. To make the dosa, the batter is spread on a hot griddle and pan-roasted until crispy.

Chicken biriyani

traditional process of Chicken Biryani starts by marinating meat in yogurt along with spices and herbs. The raw/uncooked marinated meat is layered at the bottom of a wide pot followed by another layer of par cooked basmati rice, herbs, saffron infused milk, fried onions and ghee or attar (edible essential oil).
Idli

Idli is a savory, steamed rice cake that originated in South India and is a popular breakfast food in the region. It's made from a fermented batter of de-husked black lentils and rice, and is often served with sambar and coconut chutney.
